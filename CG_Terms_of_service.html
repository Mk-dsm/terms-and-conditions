Capital Gain statement conversion service

This service provides conversion of your capital gain statement from supported trading platforms to a ITR compatible format. The service is specifically limited to converting long-term capital gains from statement from single source and where STT (security-transaction tax) is paid to a format compatible with Income Tax Return (ITR) for FY2023 and FY2024.

Supported trading platform

Currently Statement only from the following trading platform/s are supported:
  • ICICI DIRECT
  • ZERODHA
If you have different requirement do not use this service. Please ensure that the capital gains statement originates from a single bank account and is not merged with statements from other accounts or banks. We do not support statement merged from multiple sources.
Note for zerodha platform expense for transfer of shares will not come in processed file, If you want to include expense you can add it manually after process csv file is generated.

Note :

  • For Zerodha platform, expenses related to share transfers will not appear in the processed file. You can manually add these expenses after generating the CSV file.
  • Please note that the income tax website does not support appending Form 112A when uploading CSV files. If you need to include other entries in Section 112A, upload the processed CSV file first and make additional entries later.
If you have other requirement for capital gain conversion do not use the service in website and reach our support mail we will evaluate your requirement and revert when possible.
